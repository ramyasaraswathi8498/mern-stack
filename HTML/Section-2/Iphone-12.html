iPhone 12 features

Powerful Performance:

The iPhone 12 features the A14 Bionic chip, delivering blazing-fast performance for seamless multitasking and gaming.

Camera System :

With advanced camera technology, the iPhone 12 captures stunning photos and videos, even in low light conditions.

5G Connectivity:

Experience lightning-fast download and streaming speeds with 5G connectivity, enabling smoother browsing and enhanced online experiences.

Beautiful Design:

The iPhone 12 boasts a sleek and stylish design with a Ceramic Shield front cover, making it durable and resistant to scratches and cracks.

Immersive Display:

Enjoy vibrant colors and sharp details on the Super Retina XDR display, offering an immersive viewing experience for videos, photos, and games.

MagSafe Technology:

Conveniently attach MagSafe accessories like chargers and cases with magnetic alignment, ensuring secure and efficient charging.

Enhanced Privacy:

Benefit from advanced privacy features and security measures, including Face ID authentication and App Store privacy labels, ensuring your data remains secure.
